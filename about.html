About this blog

SEO Knowledge, Clearly Explained

No jargon. No fluff. Just practical search optimisation guidance you can act on.

What Athys SEO Blog Is

Athys SEO Blog is an independent resource dedicated to helping marketers, founders and website owners understand and implement effective search optimisation strategies.

Every article is written with a single goal: to take a topic that can feel complex or overwhelming and make it genuinely accessible — without dumbing it down. You'll find clear explanations, structured checklists and specific recommendations you can apply to your own site.

What We Cover

Our content focuses on four core areas of SEO:

  • Technical SEO — site structure, crawlability, indexing and performance
  • On-Page SEO — title tags, content quality, headings and internal linking
  • Core Web Vitals — page speed, responsiveness and visual stability
  • Keyword Research — finding the right search terms to target

New articles are published regularly. Subscribe to get them delivered to your inbox.

Who It's For

Whether you're managing your first website or looking to sharpen your existing SEO knowledge, Athys SEO Blog is built for people who want to understand the reasoning behind best practices — not just follow a list of rules. If you want to grow organic traffic sustainably, you're in the right place.
